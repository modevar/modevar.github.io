## Invitation to Contribute

The MODEVAR online edition will mostly focus on open discussions with the entire community to understand requirements from research & practice and discuss future directions for the development of UVL.
To get a good grasp of ongoing work in variability modeling, we invite contributions from the community showcasing their recent advances and pitfalls in the context of variability modeling.
In particular, we invite you to give short presentations (~5 minutes) regarding the following topics:

* **Tools**
    * Tools supporting UVL
    * Tools that use UVL internally
    * Tools targeting variability modeling
* **Use Cases**
    * Usage of variability modeling in practice
    * Showcases of requirements for variability specifications
    * Usages of UVL in practice

## How to Register your Presentation

If you want to give a short presentation at the MODEVAR online edition, please write us a [mail](mailto:chico.sundermann@tu-braunschweig.de) with a title and a short description (~2 sentences) on what you will present.
